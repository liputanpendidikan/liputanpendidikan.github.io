1. Expression for Introduction myself​


Hello everyone,

My name is [Your Name] and I am delighted to be here today/this evening. I would like to take a moment to introduce myself and share a bit about my background and interests.

Firstly, I come from [Your City/Country] and have spent [number of years] living and studying here. I recently graduated from [University/College] with a degree in [Your Field of Study]. During my time at university, I developed a passion for [Your Field of Interest], which I am excited to explore further in my career.

In terms of my professional experience, I have had the opportunity to work/intern at [Company/Organization] where I gained valuable skills in [Relevant Skills]. This experience allowed me to work in a team-oriented environment and develop strong communication and problem-solving abilities.

Outside of my professional life, I enjoy [Hobbies/Interests]. Whether it's reading, travelling, or playing sports, I believe it's important to maintain a well-rounded lifestyle and seek out new experiences.

In conclusion, I am thrilled to be here today/this evening and I look forward to getting to know each and every one of you. Thank you for the opportunity to introduce myself, and I am excited for what lies ahead.
