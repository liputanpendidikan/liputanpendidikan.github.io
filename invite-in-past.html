4. kalimat present tense past tense present continuous past continuous present perfect past perfect future tense be going to,modal 1,modal2 active dan passive menggunakan kata invite dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Jawaban:

Berikut ini adalah contoh kalimat-kalimat yang menggunakan kata "invite" dalam berbagai bentuk tense dan voice dalam bahasa Inggris:

Present Tense (Simple Present):

- Active: She invites her friends to the party.

- Passive: The guests are invited to the party.

Past Tense (Simple Past):

- Active: He invited me to his wedding last year.

- Passive: I was invited to his wedding last year.

Present Continuous:

- Active: They are inviting guests for the charity event.

- Passive: The guests are being invited for the charity event.

Past Continuous:

- Active: We were inviting people to join our club when it started raining.

- Passive: People were being invited to join our club when it started raining.

Present Perfect:

- Active: The company has invited a renowned speaker for the conference.

- Passive: A renowned speaker has been invited for the conference.

Past Perfect:

- Active: She had invited all her relatives to the family reunion before she left.

- Passive: All her relatives had been invited to the family reunion before she left.

Future Tense (be going to):

- Active: We are going to invite our neighbors to the barbecue party next weekend.

- Passive: Our neighbors are going to be invited to the barbecue party next weekend.

Modal Verb 1 (can):

- Active: Can you invite Sarah to the meeting tomorrow?

- Passive: Can Sarah be invited to the meeting tomorrow?

Modal Verb 2 (should):

- Active: You should invite your parents to the graduation ceremony.

- Passive: Your parents should be invited to the graduation ceremony.

10. kalimat past tense past continuous future tense modal (must) active dan passive menggunakan kata invite dalam bahasa Inggris ​


Kalimat dengan modal "must" dan "invite" dalam bahasa Inggris dengan grammar: past tense, past continuous, future tense..

1. Simple Present

(Active):

- They must invite their friends to the party.

- We must invite the clients for a business meeting.

(Passive):

- Their friends must be invited to the party.

- The clients must be invited for a business meeting.

2. Simple Past

(Active):

- She must have invited him to the wedding.

- The company must have invited them to the event.

(Passive):

- He must have been invited to the wedding by her.

- They must have been invited to the event by the company.

3. Future

(Active):

- You must invite everyone to the charity event tomorrow.

- We must invite our neighbors to the block party next week.

(Passive):

- Everyone must be invited to the charity event tomorrow.

- Our neighbors must be invited to the block party next week.

20. 3. write 10 words in past form ( simple past tense)​

Seperti: Passed, worked, cleaned

Tapi banyak juga, verb yang tidak bisa ditambah huruf2 tersebut atau bentuknya paten (verb 2 sudah ditentukan dari sananya)

29. Mau tanya tentang WH-Question Past tense. Kenapa why you invited me? bukannya why did you invite me?


why you invited me ? = karena barusan terjadi, kalau diartiin jadi -> kenapa kau mengundangku ?
why did you invited me = sudah terjadi lama, bisa juga menanyakan sebuah alasan, kalau diartiin menjadi Mengapa kau mengundangku ? (menanyakan alasan)


Coba ditelisik lagi.
Dalam "Affirmative", itu akan berbunyi ;
I was invited you. ( correct )
Bukannya :
I invited you ( Incorrect ). Tetapi, itu akan menjadi ;
I invited by you ( Correct in Passive ), yang artinya adalah Saya diundang olehmu. Jadi, secara tidak langsung yang diatas artinya tidak menjadi Saya mengundangmu. Karena, dalam bentuk "Active" dari kalimat "Passive" sebelumnya, akan menjadi :
I was invited you. ( Correct ), yang artinya akan menjadi Saya mengundangmu.

Maka, dapat disimpulkan, untuk bentuk "Interrogative", akan menjadi ;
Why you invited me ? ( Correct )
bukannya 
Why did you invite me ? 

Juga karena, yang dijadikan kata kerja lampaunya, apabila disana dituliskan secara jelas ada kata "Invited", jadi tidak perlu menggunakan "did", lagi.

34. Beni in the past ....... Beni now..... Edo and dayu in the past ... Edo and dayu now...


Beni ini the past ( He was....)

Beni now ( He is....)

Edo and Dayu ini the past ( They were.... or Edo dan Dayu were...)

Edo dan Dayu now ( They are.... or Edo dan Bayu are....)

Note :
Kata "was" digunakan untuk 1 orang di masa lalu.
Kata "were" digunakan untuk lebih dari 1 orang di masa lalu
*Kata "is" digunakan untuk 1 orang di masa sekarang atau yang sedang berlangsung saat ini
*Kata "are" digunakan untuk lebih dari 1 orang di masa sekarang atau yang sedang berlangsung saat ini

36. ll.fill in the blank by using passive voice1.the instrument.... (buy)many years ago. past2.many people.... (understand) by their boss. simple future3.a good boy... (invite)in wedding party past perfectplease tolong di jawabn Cepat ​


1. The Instrument was bought many years ago.

2. Many people will be understood by their boss.

3. A good boy had been invited in wedding party.

PEMBAHASAN

Passive Voice

↪adalah sebuah jenis kalimat dimana kedudukan subjek berpindah menjadi penerima aksi, sedangkan kedudukan objek berpindah menjadi si pemberi aksi.

➡Penjelasan

1. The instrument was bought many years ago.↘⬇↙

Terjemahan: Instrumen itu dibeli beberapa tahun lalu.The instrument > Subjek singular (was)Kalimat ini merupakan jenis Past Tense.Rumus: S + Was/were + V3 + O

2. Many people will be understood by their boss.↘⬇↙

Terjemahan: Banyak orang akan dimengerti oleh bos mereka.Kalimat ini merupakan jenis Future Tense.Rumus: S + Will + Be + V3 + O

3. A good boy had been invited in wedding party.↘⬇↙

Terjemahan: Laki2 yang baik telah diundang di pesta pernikahan.Kalimat ini merupakan jenis Past Perfect Tense.Rumus: S + Had + Been + V3 + O
