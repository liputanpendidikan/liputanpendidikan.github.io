1. caritakan cerita berjudul "bayangan dalam cerita"​


Jawaban:

Judul Cerita: "Bayangan dalam Cerita"

Alur:

Cerita ini dimulai dengan seorang penulis bernama Adrian, yang selalu menulis cerita-cerita fantastis dan misterius. Suatu hari, ketika dia sedang berjalan-jalan di hutan yang terpencil, dia secara tak sengaja menemukan buku tua yang tampak sangat kuno. Buku tersebut tidak memiliki judul atau penulis yang jelas, tetapi halaman-halamannya berisi cerita-cerita yang sangat menarik.

Adrian mulai membaca buku tersebut dan merasa seperti dia terbawa masuk ke dalam cerita-cerita itu. Setiap cerita yang dia baca membuatnya merasakan sensasi yang nyata, seolah-olah dia berada di dalam dunia cerita tersebut. Dia bertemu dengan karakter-karakter dalam buku itu, mengalami petualangan-petualangan yang menegangkan, dan merasakan emosi mereka.

Namun, semakin dalam Adrian terperdeepalam buku itu, semakin dia merasa bahwa buku tersebut memiliki kekuatan magis yang mengendalikannya. Dia merasa bahwa buku itu menciptakan bayangan-bayangan dari cerita-cerita itu yang mulai mengikuti dan mengganggunya dalam kehidupan nyata.

Adrian merasa tertekan oleh bayangan-bayangan ini dan mencoba mencari cara untuk keluar dari pengaruh buku itu. Dia mulai menggali sejarah buku tersebut dan menemukan bahwa buku itu pernah dimiliki oleh seorang penyihir jahat yang menciptakan cerita-cerita dalamnya untuk mencapai tujuan jahatnya.

Dengan bantuan seorang arkeolog yang ahli dalam ilmu supranatural, Adrian mencoba mengatasi kekuatan buku tersebut dan membebaskan dirinya dari bayangan-bayangan yang menghantuinya. Ini adalah perjalanan yang menegangkan dan penuh dengan intrik misterius.

Akhir cerita mengungkapkan bahwa Adrian berhasil mengalahkan pengaruh buku itu, tetapi buku tersebut harus dimusnahkan untuk menghindari bahaya lebih lanjut. Cerita ini menggambarkan perjalanan Adrian dalam menghadapi kekuatan magis yang menakutkan dan mengungkapkan bahwa kreativitas dan imajinasi juga bisa menjadi kekuatan yang berbahaya jika tidak dikelola dengan bijaksana.

25. Cerita dongeng yang menarik dan berbahasa inggris dan tidak terlalu panjang (2cerita)


The Greedy Boy

There lived a twin brother called Joko and Joni. They were an identical twins, even their mother sometimes found it too hard to differentiate them. Although they were a twins, they were very different in everything when it comes to their appearance. They never agreed with anything.

Joko had no friends, whereas for Joni the world was friendship. Joko loved sweet foods and Joni loved to eat spicy foods. Joko loved his mother so much and Joni loved his father more. While Joko was generous, Joni was greedy.

While they grew up, their father called them to talk about something. Their father wanted to share his wealth equally to them. Joni did not agree with the agreement. He argued that he is more strong and intelligent than Joko. He asked for higher wealth to his father.

Then, their father arranged a competition between them to decided who could get the higher wealth. He ordered his twin sons to walk as far as they can and they had to return home before the sunset. Whoever walked the longer distance and returned home on time would get huge amount of his wealth proportionate to distance they walked. Their father did not give the watch to calculate time.

Joko and Joni started to walk during a sunny day. Joko walked slowly but steadily, while Joni started to run as fas as he could.

Joko predicted that the distance he cover until mid-noon must be equal to the distance he would reach house before sunset. When mid-noon came, Joko decided to return back so he could back home on time.

However, Joni, with his greediness to earn more wealth, did not stop running even after mid-noon. He ran two times longer than the distance Joko was covered. Then, he decided to return back to home. He ran as fast as he could but the Sun had already turned orange. Unfortunately, he could not return to home on time. The sun had already set in his half way to home.

Joni was defeated because of his greediness!

Moral Value : GREEDINESS WILL MAKE YOU LOSE EVERYTHING
