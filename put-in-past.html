PEMBAHASAN

SIMPLE PAST

➡ Tenses yang menjelaskan suatu kejadian yang terjadi pada masa lampau. Simple past terbagi menjadi 2 macam yaitu :

Simple past verbalSimple past nominal

Simple Past Verbal

Rumus :

(+) S + V2 + O

We studied English. She ate fried rice.

➡ Pada soal di atas yang termasuk ke dalam kalimat simple past verbal positif terdapat pada soal nomor 1 sampai 5, 9 sampai 14, dan 18 sampai 19. Untuk mengetahui perubahan verb-2 dari kata kerja yang digunakan pada soal di atas kita bisa mencarinya di daftar regular verbs (kata kerja beraturan) ataupun irregular verbs (kata kerja tidak beraturan). Dan berikut ini adalah perubahan kata kerja pada nomor yang telah di sebutkan :

Regular verbs

Stay => Stayed => StayedWant => Wanted => WantedStart => Started => Started

Irregular verbs

Have => Had => HadDrive => Drove => DrivenBreak => Broke => BrokenSpend => Spent => SpentGet => Got => GottenFind => Found => FoundSee => Saw => SeenGo => Went => GoneBuy => Bought => BoughtLeave => Left => Left

(-) S + Did + not + V1 + O

We did not study English. She did not eat fried rice.

➡ Yang termasuk kalimat simple past verbal negative pada soal di atas tertera pada nomor 6, 8 dan 15

Untuk nomor 6 karena menggunakan modal "can" maka bentuk past dari "can" adalah "could". Jadi bentuk negatif nya menjadi could not.

(?) Did + S + V1 + O

Did we study English? Did she eat fried rice?

Simple Past Nominal

Rumus :

(+) S + To be (was/were) + complement

They were classmates. He was an actor two years ago?

➡ Yang termasuk simple past nominal bentuk positif terdapat pada soal nomor 7 dan 16-17.

Pada soal nomor 7 menggunakan to be were karena subject nya "they". Pada soal nomor 16 menggunakan to be was karena subject nya "it". Pada soal nomor 17 menggunakan to be was karena subject nya kata ganti benda tunggal "the weather".

(-) S + To be (was/were) + not + complement

They were not classmates. He was not an actor two years ago.

(?) To be (was/were) + S + complement

Were they classmates? Was he an actor two years ago? Learn more about :

Help => Helped

Watch => Watched

Play => Played

Listen => Listened

Walk => Walked

Talk => Talked

Visit => Visited

Tambahan -d

Love => Loved

Live => Lived

Taste => Tasted

Arrive => Arrived

Smile => Smiled

Tambahan -ied

Jika kata kerjanya berakhiran huruf -y dan sebelum huruf -y ada huruf mati, maka -y dihilangkan dan langsung ditambah -ied.

Try => Tried

Study => Studied

PEMBAHASAN:

Simple Past Tense

Simple past tense digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian di waktu tertentu di masa lampau.

Time expression: yesterday, last two days, last year, this morning, a week ago, ...etc.

Formula:

Verbal sentences:

(+)  Subject + Verb2

(-)   Subject + did + not + Verb1  

(?)  Did + Subject + Verb1?

Nominal sentence:

(+)  Subject + to be (was/were) + O/C

(-)   Subject + to be (was/ were) + O/C  

(?)  To be (was/ were) +Subject + O/C?

Contoh :

1. (+) Susi took a bath yesterday

  (-) Susi did not take a bath yesterday

  (?) Did Susi take a bath yesterday?

2. (+) Susi was an artist

    (-) Susi was not an artist

    (?) Was Susi an artist?

3. (+) Susi brought her dictionary  

    (-) Susi did not bring her dictionary  

    (?) Did Susi bring her dictionary ?

 

4. (+) My father drank a cup of coffee this morning

    (-) My father did not drink a cup of coffee this morning

    (?) Did My father drink a cup of coffee this morning?

5. (+) Amy swam in the swimming pool yesterday

   (-) Amy did not swim in the swimming pool yesterday

   (?) Did Amy swim in the swimming pool yesterday?

6. (+) you went to bed at 9 last night

    (-) you did not go to bed at 9 last night

    (?)Did you go to bed at 9 last night?

7. (+) She bought dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (-) She did not buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago

   (?) Did She buy dresses in that supermarket two days ago?

8. (+) My teacher wrote with a pen, but we wrote with pencils  

   (-) My teacher did not write with a pen, but we did not write with pencils  

   (?) Did My teacher write with a pen, but did we write with pencils?

9.(+) My mom was very diligent

   (-) My mom was not very diligent

   (?) Was My mom very diligent?

10. (+) My father was in the office yesterday afternoon  

     (-) My father was not in the office yesterday afternoon  

     (?) Was My father in the office yesterday afternoon?  

Past Perfect Tense

Past perfect tense adalah suatu tense yang menggambarkan suatu kejadian yang terjadi sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau dan telah selesai sebelum kejadian lain yang terjadi di masa lampau juga. Tense ini terdiri dari gabungan aspek perfect (sebelum satu spesifik waktu di masa lampau) dan bingkai waktu past (masa lampau).

Rumus Past Perfect Tense

Tense ini dibentuk dengan bentuk masa lampau dari auxiliary verb have atau has, yaitu had dan past participle (verb 3). Semua subjek baik singular atau plural menggunakan had.

Positif

Subjek +  had + verb 3  

Contoh :

They had lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time He had learned English for one year

Negatif

Subjek +  had + not + verb 3  

Contoh:

They had not lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time He had not learned English for one year

Interogatif

Had+ subjek + verb 3?  

Contoh:

Had they lived in Kuala Simapang for a long time Had he learned English for one year?
