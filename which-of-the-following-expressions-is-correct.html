5. Which of the following is the CORRECT sequence of producing Faber-Castell Erasure?​


Jawaban:

The correct sequence of producing Faber-Castell Erasure is as follows:

1. Raw Material Acquisition: The process begins with the acquisition of raw materials required for making erasers, such as rubber and other additives.

2. Mixing and Formulation: The raw materials are mixed and formulated in specific ratios to achieve the desired properties and consistency of the eraser compound.

3. Compounding: The compound is then processed further using specialized equipment to create a homogeneous mixture and ensure proper dispersion of additives.

4. Molding: The compound is molded into the desired eraser shape using molds or extrusion techniques. The molds may have various designs or sizes depending on the intended use of the eraser.

5. Curing: The molded erasers are then subjected to a curing process, which involves applying heat or chemical treatments to solidify and stabilize the eraser material.

6. Quality Control: The erasers undergo thorough quality control checks to ensure they meet the required standards in terms of performance, appearance, and durability.

7. Packaging: The approved erasers are packaged into suitable packaging materials, which may include individual wrappers or bulk packaging, depending on the intended market.

8. Distribution: The packaged erasers are then distributed to wholesalers, retailers, or directly to consumers through various channels.

It's important to note that the specific manufacturing process for Faber-Castell erasers may vary based on their specific product lines and variations. The above sequence provides a general overview of the typical steps involved in producing erasers.


6. Which one of the following expressions is regarded as greeting?


Pertanyaan diatas kurang lengkap karena tidak menyertakan pilihan jawaban untuk pertanyaan tersebut. Berikut pertanyaan lengkap beserta jawabannya.

Which one of the following expressions is regarded as greeting?

a. Thank you

b. I’m fine, thanks

c. Good morning

d. Good bye

Answer: C

Penjelasan:

Greeting expression adalah ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menyapa seseorang sebelum memulai percakapan.

Contoh greeting yang digunakan dalam kehidupan sehari-hari:

- Hi

- Hello

- Good morning/ afternoon/ evening

- How are you?

Contoh greeting yang digunakan pada saat tertentu:

- Merry Christmas (diucapkan ketika hari raya Natal)

- Happy new year (diucapkan ketika tahun baru)

- Happy birthday (diucapkan ketika seseorang berulah tahun)

- Happy Vesak day (diucapkan ketika hari raya waisak)

- Happy Eid Al-Fitr (diucapkan ketika hari raya Idul Fitri)

Semoga membantu.

31. which arrangement is the correct order of the day?


The correct order of the day in a week :

Monday (Senin)Tuesday (Selasa)Wednesday (Rabu)Thursday (Kamis)Friday (Jum'at)Saturday (Sabtu)Sunday (Minggu)

Pembahasan

In a week, there are seven days.  Monday, tuesday, wednesday, thursday, friday, saturday, sunday.

In a month, there are four weeks.

In a year, there are twelve months. January, February, March, April, May, June, July, August, September, October, November, December.

What day is today ? (hari apa hari ini?)

Today is Saturday (hari ini adalah hari sabtu)

What day is tomorrow ? (hari apa besok?)

Tomorrow is Sunday (besok adalah hari minggu)

If today is Tuesday, tomorrow is Wednesday (jika hari ini selasa, maka besok adalah hari Rabu)

39. which of the following expressions asks for opinion​


Pertanyaan diatas kurang lengkap karena tidak menyertakan pilihan jawaban untuk pertanyaan tersebut. Berikut pertanyaan lengkap beserta jawabannya.

Which of the following expressions asks for opinion ?

a. I think we should study harder from now on

b. sally, what did you say about the math test today?

c. Handi, do you want to go to the concert with me ?

d. I hope I have the right time that to see it

e. I am sorry

Answer: B. sally, what did you say about the math test today?

Jawaban A merupakan bentuk expression of giving opinion, jawaban C merupakan bentuk expression of invitation, jawaban D merupakan bentuk expression of hope, sedangkan jawaban E merupakan bentuk expression of apology.

Link untuk soal lengkapnya akan disertakan pada bagian pelajari lebih lanjut.

Penjelasan:

Asking for opinion merupakan suatu ungkapan yang digunakan untuk menanyakan pendapat orang lain. Contoh expression of asking opinion:

- What do you think about …?

- How do you feel about …?

- What do you say about …?

- Do/ don't you think …?

- What’s your idea on …?

- What’s your view on …?

Untuk menjawab expression of asking opinion dapat digunakan expressionn of giving opinion, berikut contohnya.

- I think …

- I feel …

- In my opinion, …

- I believe that …

- For me, …
