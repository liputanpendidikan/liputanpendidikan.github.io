19. Soal nya: Put the verbs in the brackets into the correct tenses. Materinya tentang “The Past Perfect Tense”


9. The man had comitted crime before the judge finally sentenced him to jail

10. They had strived hard before they eventually got success

11. We had considered it carefully before we made the decision

12. He had had breakfast before he left for the office

13. Father left the office after he had done all his work

14. After he had approached her nicely she told us that she was ready to join us

15. The supporters had arrived long before their favorite team began the competition

Past perfect digunakan untuk kejadian yang terjadi lebih awal (the earliest event).

Simple past digunakan untuk kejadian yang terjadi setelahnya (the next most recent event).

(+) Subjek +  had + verb 3

(-) Subjek +  had + not + verb 3

(?) Had+ subjek + verb 3?

Rumus Past Perfect Tense. Tense ini dibentuk dengan bentuk masa lampau dari auxiliary verb have atau has, yaitu had dan past participle (verb 3). Semua subjek baik singular atau plural menggunakan had.
1. had commited
2.had strived
sisanya tinggal nambahin had + v3
