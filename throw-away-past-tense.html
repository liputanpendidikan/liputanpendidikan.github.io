7. Buatlah - past continue (5) - past tense (5) - present tense (5) - past tense + while + past continue - past continue + when + past tense - while * past continue + past tense - when + past tende + past continue


PAST TENSE VS PAST CONTINOUS  Jawaban    Pendahuluan  

Simple past tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung di waktu lampau. Keterangan waktu yang menunjukan waktu lampau dibutuhkan disini, seperti: yesterday, last week, two days ago, two hours ago, last night, dan lain lain.

Rumus simple past adalah:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O?

Past Continuous Tense adalah tense yang digunakan untuk menyatakan kejadian yang berlangsung dan mempunyai proses di masa lampau. Disini penggunaan kata when dan while sering digunakan ketika past continuous tense bertembu simple past tense.  

Rumus Past continuous adalah:

(+) S + was/were + Ving + O

(-) S + was/were not + Ving + O

(?) Was/were + S + Ving + O?

was = I, He, She, It

were = You, They, We

Pembahasan  

 Buatlah kalimat:

Past Continuous:

I was watching TV at the same time yesterday.Mrs. Susan was teaching us yesterday at 9 am.They were playing football here yesterday at 3 pmWe were visiting Tina yesterday afternoon.My father was working at the same time yesterday.

Simple Past Tense

I bought a new car last monthWe won the game yesterday.They were the champion of Indonesian League last season.Rina visited her grandmother last week.He was the victim of earthquake in Palu.

Simple Present Tense

I always go to school at 6.30 amThe sun rises from the east.Where do you come from?I don't know what you mean.She plays piano beautifully.

Past Continuous + while + Past Continuous

My father was washing his car while my mother was sweeping the floor.

Past Continuous + when + past tense

I was playing video game when you came.

While + Past Continuous + Past Tense

While I was daydreaming in the class, Mrs. Susan called my name.

When + Past Tense + Past Continuous

When my mother knocked the door, I was sleeping.

PEMBAHASAN MATERI

ʕ Simple Past Tense ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang terjadi atau dilakukan di masa yang telah berlalu.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + Verb 2 + Object/Complement(–) Subject + didn't + Verb 1 + Object/Complement(?) Did + Subject + Verb 1 + Object/Complement + ?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Continuous ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang sedang terjadi atau berlangsung di masa yang telah berlalu. Misalnya: Saya sedang menulis berita saat kamu datang kemarin.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + was/were + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(–) Subject + was/were + not + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(?) Was/Were + Subject + Verb-ing + Object/Complement + ?

ᴥ Notes:

Was, digunakan pada subject: I, he, she, dan it. Were, digunakan pada subject: You, we, dan they.

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Future ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang akan terjadi namun dengan persepsi masa lalu.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + would + Verb 1 + Object/Complement(–) Subject + wouldn't + Verb 1 + Object/Complement(?) Would + Subject + Verb 1 + Object/Complement + ?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Perfect ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang telah terjadi di masa lalu di mana waktu terjadinya dijelaskan secara spesifik. Misalnya: Aku telah menyelesaikan tugas sekolah ku sebelum Ibu menelpon ku.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + had + Verb 3 + Object/Complement(–) Subject + hadn't + Verb 3 + Object/Complement(?) Had + Subject + Verb 3 + Object/Complement + ?

◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌◌

ʕ Simple Past Perfect Continuous ʔ

ᴥ Fungsi

Mengungkapkan suatu peristiwa/kegiatan yang telah terjadi di masa lalu di mana kehiatan tersebut masih berlangsung di masa tersebut.

ᴥ Pattern:

(+) Subject + had + been + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(–) Subject + hadn't + been + Verb-ing + Object/Complement(?) Had + Subject + been + Verb-ing + Object/Complement + ?

22. perbedan(throw away)dan(throw out)pls​


Jawaban:

They both have multiple meanings. The one they have in common is to "get rid of something because it is useless or garbage".

throw away

1.

a. To get rid of as useless: threw away yesterday's newspaper.

b. (Games) To discard: threw away two aces.

2.

a. To fail to take advantage of: threw away a chance to make a fortune.

b. To waste or use in a foolish way: threw away her inheritance.

3. To utter or perform in an offhand, seemingly careless way: The play's villain throws away the news that the house has burned down.

throw out

1. To give off; emit: searchlights throwing out powerful beams.

2. To reject or discard: The committee threw out her proposal.

3. To get rid of as useless: threw out the garbage.

4. Informal To offer, as a suggestion or plan: They sat around throwing out names of people they might want to invite to the party.

5. To force to leave a place or position, especially in an abrupt or unexpected manner: The convicted judge was thrown out of office. The headwaiter threw the disorderly guest out.

6.

a. To disengage (a clutch, for example).

b. To put out of alignment: threw my back out.

7. Baseball To put out (a base runner) by throwing the ball to the player guarding the base to which the base runner is moving

26. Contoh cerita menggunakan simple past tense past continous tense dan past perfect tense


Pembahasan:


Sebelum membuat cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense, kita sebaiknya mengetahui struktur kalimat yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense terlebih dahulu. Berikut penjelasannya.



Simple past tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk mengekspresikan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang dilakukan pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + V2 + O

(-) S + didn't / did not + V1 + O

(?) Did + S + V1 + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I watched the World Cup on TV last night.



Past continuous tense

Tenses ini digunakan menyatakan suatu kejadian atau kegiatan yang sedang berlangsung di masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + was / were + V-ing + O

(-) S + wasn't / weren't + V-ing + O

(?) Was / were + S + V-ing + O + ?

Contoh kalimat: I was cooking dinner when you called me yesterday


Past perfect tense

Tenses ini digunakan untuk menyatakan suatu kejadian yang telah terjadi sebelum kejadian lain pada masa lampau.

Struktur kalimat:

(+) S + had + V3 + O

(-) S + had + not + V3 + O

(?) Had + S + V3 + O +?

Contoh kalimat: She had had breakfast before she went to school.


Berikut contoh cerita yang menggunakan simple past tense, past continuous tense, dan past perfect tense.


Last week was my birthday. My family members seemed like they forgot my birthday. They didn't say happy birthday or gave a birthday present for me. They just did their daily routine as usual like there was nothing special. At school, my friends also didn't remember my birthday. It made me so sad. I went home early after the class had finished. I went to my bedroom when i had arrived at home. I cried because i thought nobody remembered my birthday. Suddenly, my bedroom's door opened when i was crying. I saw my family and my friends brought a birthday cake for me. I wiped my tears when they were singing happy birthday. Apparently, they had secretly planned to trick me because they knew that my birthday was coming soon. it was an unforgettable birthday surprise for me.

34. Rosa runs away from her house ubah ke simple past tense


The Answer:

Bentuk lampau (Simple Past Tense) dari kalimat di soal adalah Rosa ran away from her house.

Explanation

Perintah soal adalah untuk mengubah kata kerja yang ada di kalimat yang dimaksud di soal menjadi kata kerja bentuk kedua (Verb 2), yang mana digunakan dalam bentuk kata kerja Simple Past Tense.

Simple Past Tense adalah suatu bentuk kata kerja (Tense) dalam Bahasa Inggris yang digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta terhadap masa lalu, tindakan dan kebiasaan yang dilakukan pada masa lalu.

Struktur kalimat verba berpola Simple Past Tense adalah sebagai berikut.

(+) Subject + Verb 2

(-) Subject + didn't + Verb 2

(?) Did + Subject + Verb 2

Keterangan:

Subject = Subjek kalimat.Verb 2 = Kata kerja bentuk kedua.Did = Kata kerja bantu 'do' bentuk lampau.Didn't = Bentuk negatif dari 'did'.

—

Contoh kalimat berpola Simple Past Tense adalah sebagai berikut.

She did the dishes this morning. [Dia mencuci piring pagi ini]She did not do the dishes this morning. [Dia tidak mencuci piring pagi ini]Did she do the dishes this morning? [Apakah dia mencuci piring pagi ini?]We played basketball yesterday afternoon. [Kami bermain bola basket kemarin sore]We did not play basketball yesterday afternoon. [Kami tidak bermain bola basket kemarin sore]Did we play basketball yesterday afternoon? [Apakah kita bermain bola basket kemarin?]Gina and I went to a supermarket a week ago. [Gina dan aku pergi ke sebuah supermarket seminggu yang lalu]Gina and I didn't go to a supermarket a week ago. [Gina dan aku tidak pergi ke sebuah supermarket seminggu yang lalu]Did Gina and I go to a supermarket a week ago? [Apakah Gina dan aku pergi ke sebuah supermarket seminggu yang lalu?]

35. apa saja masalah yang muncul dalam pembuatan prototipe throw away​


Masalah yang muncul dalam pembuatan Prototipe Throw Away.

Biaya. Pembuatan prototipe throw away membutuhkan biaya yang cukup besar karena prototipe tersebut hanya digunakan sekali dan dibuang setelah selesai digunakan.Waktu. Pembuatan prototipe throw away membutuhkan waktu yang relatif lama karena harus merancang dan memproduksi prototipe dari awal.Keterbatasan desain. Karena prototipe throw away dibuat dengan tujuan untuk digunakan sekali dan dibuang, maka desainnya mungkin lebih sederhana dan kurang kompleks dibandingkan dengan prototipe yang dirancang untuk digunakan dalam jangka waktu yang lebih lama.Kurangnya umpan balik. Dalam pembuatan prototipe throw away, umpan balik atau masukan dari pengguna mungkin tidak tersedia karena prototipe tersebut hanya digunakan sekali dan dibuang.Penggunaan bahan yang tidak ramah lingkungan. Karena prototipe throw away dibuang setelah selesai digunakan, maka bahan yang digunakan untuk membuat prototipe tersebut mungkin tidak ramah lingkungan dan dapat menimbulkan dampak negatif bagi lingkungan.
