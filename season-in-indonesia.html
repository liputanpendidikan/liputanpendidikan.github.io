11. text deskripsi b inggris musim di indonesia(season in indonesia)​


Jawaban :

Indonesia is located in the tropics, Indonesia only has 2 seasons, the rainy season and the dry season.

The Dry Season :

The dry season in Indonesia mostly takes place between April-September. During the dry season, the humidity tends to be very low. For the dry season occurs when the wind blows east monsoon.

The Rainy Season :

The rainy season in Indonesia lasts between October-February. Heavy rainfall often results in floods and it happened in some areas in Indonesia. For the rainy season mainly occurs when the wind blows west monsoon.
