17. At this time yesterday Reny and I were paying a call our classmate. What tense it is?


Jawaban:

The sentence "At this time yesterday Reny and I were paying a call our classmate" is in the past continuous tense. This tense is used to describe an action that was ongoing or in progress at a specific point in the past. In this case, it describes what Reny and you were doing at the same time on the previous day.

Jawaban:

past continuous tense

Penjelasan:

terdapat kata kata "were playing"

bentuk past continuous tense sendiri adalah

I/he/she/it + was + verb ing.
We/you/they + were + verb ing.

31. Today is sunday what day was it yesterday


Jawaban:

Sunday = minggu

Yesterday = kemarin

jadi jwbannya = Saturday /sabtu

Today is Sunday. What day was it yesterday?

The Answer:

It was Saturday.

Explanation

Diketahui,

Today is Sunday. [Hari ini hari Minggu]

Ditanya,

What day was it yesterday? [Hari apakah kemarin?]

Jawab:

Karena hari sebelum hari Minggu (Sunday) adalah hari Sabtu (Saturday), maka jawabannya adalah hari Sabtu (Saturday).
