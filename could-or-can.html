24. He or she could tell you. Predicatenya adalah?


Predikat adalah kata kerja. Jadi, predikat kalimat tersebut adalah "tell".

Predicate is part of a statement that says about subject.

(Predikat adalah bagian dari pernyataan yang menerangkan tentang subjek).


=> He or she could tell you.


Predikatnya adalah "could tell" (modality + V1).


Keterangan tambahan :

Subject : He or she (pronoun/kata ganti milik).

Object : You (pronoun/kata ganti milik).

39. He or she could tell you. Predicatenya adalah?


The word "could" is part of modal auxiliary. It gives the more detail information in the sentence.

Then the word " tell" is a verb or we can call it as the predicate.

The word "he / she" is part of subject in the sentence.
