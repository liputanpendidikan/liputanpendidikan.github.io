22. Use the folowing adjectives incomparing things or persons Careful,sweet,industrious,tall,expensive


Penjelasan:

Comparing Things:

Careful vs. reckless: He is careful with his money, unlike his reckless friend who spends without thinking.

Sweet vs. sour: This sweet fruit is much better than the sour one I had yesterday.

Industrious vs. lazy: The industrious worker finished the project ahead of schedule, while the lazy worker barely made any progress.

Tall vs. short: The tall building can be seen from miles away, unlike the short one next to it.

Expensive vs. cheap: The expensive restaurant was too fancy for my taste, so I opted for a cheaper one instead.

Comparing Persons:

Careful vs. careless: She is careful with her belongings, unlike her careless sister who always loses things.

Sweet vs. sour: The sweet-natured person always tries to be kind, unlike the sour one who is always grumpy.

Industrious vs. lazy: The industrious employee always goes above and beyond, unlike the lazy one who does the bare minimum.

Tall vs. short: The tall basketball player can reach the basket easily, unlike the short player who struggles.

Expensive vs. cheap: The expensive shopper always buys designer brands, unlike the cheap one who looks for bargains
